Mission

Well Connected Now aspires to rebrand mental health, and make trustworthy support more accessible, especially for millennial professionals and entrepreneurs facing career & life challenges.

Matches specially selected by clients' circumstances, budget, & preferences. Referrals encouraged. Telehealth options available.

  • Cross-Industry Mentors

    Strategize your next career steps with a specially matched mentor.

  • Business Coaches

    Strive towards your goals with an experienced, certified coach.

  • Therapists

    Improve your relationships with discreet, specially selected therapists.

How it Works

1

WCN Consultation

Confidential call discussing circumstances, budget, and preferences with trained peer counselor, Lisa Abdilova.

2

Personal Match

WCN specially matches you with a pre-vetted mentor, coach, or therapist--whoever could be most effective at addressing your current career & life challenges.

3

Introduction

After your WCN match confirms their availability, we facilitate a warm email introduction, and share some initial background for you to build on in your next consultation.

4

Compatibility Check

You'll want a mentor, coach, or therapist you can wholeheartedly trust and respect. Before WCN, that took most professionals countless tries to find.

WCN guarantees match satisfaction. Does conversation flow easily? Can you be yourself? If not, we'll find you someone else!
