Well Connected Now aspires to rebrand mental health, and make trustworthy support more accessible, especially for millennial professionals and entrepreneurs facing career & life challenges.

How it Works

1

WCN Consultation

Confidential call discussing circumstances, budget, and preferences with trained peer counselor, Lisa Abdilova.

2

Personal Match

WCN specially matches you with a pre-vetted mentor, coach, or therapist--whoever could be most effective at addressing your current career & life challenges.

3

Introduction

After your WCN match confirms their availability, we facilitate a warm email introduction, and share some initial background for you to build on in your next consultation.

4

Compatibility Check

Before WCN, it took most professionals 3-5 tries to find a specialist they could trust & respect enough to follow their advice. WCN guarantees a better match faster. If something feels off, we'll find you someone else!

Sample Matches

Professionals

  • Ambitious, intellectually curious, and resourceful
  • 20-30somethings navigating corporate & start-up careers
  • Entrepreneurs / Self-Employed / Service Providers

Professional Client sample

  • Somewhere on the spectrum of professionally dissatisfied to miserable
  • Want more social support and/or a healthier lifestyle - the 2 strongest resilience factors for overcoming challenges

Mentors

  • Accomplished & warm, with broad variety of experience levels and industries
  • Enjoy helping sort through personal & professional challenges
  • Happy to share own takeaways & missteps to support your growth

Mentor Match Sample

  • Assess circumstances for whether standard to industry cult
  • Learn how other roles in different organizations compare
  • Consider advice on strategic career and business moves

Coaches

  • 10+ years of relevant industry experience (i.e. former executives, start-up founders, corporate marketers..)
  • 2Professionally  trained to listen & identify your values, passions, and motivators
  • Provide encouragement, resources, & accountability structures to achieve your dream goals, without any conflicts of interest

Coach Match sample

  • Explore new career directions with more meaning, impact, or balance
  • Expand business skillset (ie start-ups, management, sales, recruiting..)
  • Strive towards your dream goals with an industry-experienced supporter

Therapist

  • Practical, tech-savvy, and  non-judgmental
  • Specialize in mild to moderate professional & life challenges
  • Professionally trained in most effective evidence-based approaches

Therapist Match Sample

  • Confidentially confront personal challenges
  • Positively reframe negative, self-defeating thoughts
  • Investigate the root of your current life or career dissatisfaction
