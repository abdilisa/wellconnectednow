WCN Coaching Mission

Feeling Stuck?

When you're dissatisfied with your life, career, or business progress, the ambiguity around the roots of your challenges and how to overcome them can be overwhelming.

Coaches right for you?

WCN personally matches you to business coaches with just the right experience and skillset to empower you to realize your options, and confidently achieve your goals.

How it Works

1

WCN Consultation


Professional interested in meeting a WCN Coach after consulting trained peer counselor, Lisa Abdilova, on circumstances, preferences, and budget.

2

Coach Match Requests

When a coach's specialties and background match the professional's ideal, we'll check on their availability and privately share some general context.

3

Coach Match Intro


After the coach and professional both opt-in, WCN sends a warm introduction email with more details for them to build on in their conversations.

4

Coach Consultation


Meet in person, over the phone, or via video chat for initial free 30 minute consultation. Discuss professional's goals, coach's approach, and program package options. Determine whether interested in moving forward.

5

Complete Coaching Program

  • Typically meet bi-weekly for 2-3 months; frequency depending on goals.
  • Professionally assess personal/career values, motivators, skill-sets, & career options.
  • Confidentially tackle challenging executive, business, and personal decisions alongside experienced supporters without any conflicts of interest.
